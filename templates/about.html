CRUD Operations Instruction

Create

Steps to Create:
  1. Navigate to the "Create" section of the application.
  2. Fill out the necessary fields with the required information.
  3. Click the "Submit" or "Save" button to create the new data entry.

Read

Steps to Read:
  1. Navigate to the "Read" or "View" section of the application.
  2. Search or browse for the data entry you want to view.
  3. Click on the entry to view its details.
  4. You should see all the information associated with the selected data entry.

Update

Steps to Update:
  1. Navigate to the "Update" or "Edit" section of the application.
  2. Search for the data entry you want to update.
  3. Click on the entry to open the editing interface.
  4. Modify the necessary fields with the updated information.
  5. Click the "Update" or "Save Changes" button to apply the changes.
  6. You should receive a confirmation message indicating that the data has been successfully updated.

Delete

Steps to Delete:
  1. Navigate to the "Delete" or "Remove" section of the application.
  2. Search for the data entry you want to delete.
  3. Select the entry by checking the checkbox next to it.
  4. Click the "Delete" or "Remove" button to initiate the deletion process.
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  6. You should receive a confirmation message indicating that the data has been successfully deleted.
